Swiss Climate Challenge

Thank you for being part of our journey.

Swiss Climate Challenge has officially concluded as of December 2024. We are deeply grateful for all your contributions in making a positive impact on our environment.

Together, we tracked emissions, took meaningful steps to reduce our carbon footprint, and showed the power of collective action. Though our app is no longer active, the mission to fight climate change continues.

We encourage you to stay engaged and keep making sustainable choices for our planet.

For more information or to find other sustainability initiatives from Swisscom, please visit swisscom.ch/sustainability.

If you are a company interested in taking advantage of digital sustainability solutions, please visit Data-driven sustainability for businesses.

Thank you again for your dedication and support.

— The Swiss Climate Challenge Team
